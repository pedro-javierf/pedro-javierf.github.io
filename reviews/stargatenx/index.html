StargateNX Review

Posted on 2018, Aug 21 4 mins read

Welcome back to another post! Today, I will be reviewing StargateNX, a new paid homebrew software for the switch and the SXPro.

What is it?

StargateNX is a new software solution provided as a plugin for SX Installer that allows you to download and install different packages for the console. Developed by the stargate team, it follows a kind of SAAS (Software as a Service) model, with a monthly subscription. At the moment of this analysis, it requires the SX OS custom firmware, and won’t work with other custom firmwares yet.

Official Specs

Direct download ALL available content for Nintendo Switch Zero risk of a ban! (w/ SX OS stealth mode) Even if your Switch is already banned by Nintendo, our product still works perfectly!

Where can it be obtained and for how much?

Quoting StargateNX team:

StargateNX is for sale now for $24.95/month from the resellers listed here or from your favorite local reseller.

SetUp and first contact

This is a brief review, pros and cons, of StargateNX.

Firstly, we have to set up our console and microSD card properly. You need SX OS 2.5.2 or higher. I have tested with 2.5.3. SX Installer must be placed in the homebrew folder of the card. The StargateNX .zip file must also be decompressed to the microSD card. That’s everything required for it to work.

The console must be connected to internet, and SX OS Stealth Mode must be enabled. The next step is to Launch SX Installer. To do this, launch SX OS (either emunand or sysnand), click on the “Album” application, and move to the “Homebrew” menu. You should see the SX Installer icon, press A to launch it.

Metrics

Is this new software fast and stable? My verdict is: YES.

While testing downloads, the servers responded in a very robust way. Download speed was the maximum available with my current internet connection (100mpbs, with the console reaching 7.5 Mb/s using the 5G WiFi connection). Downloads have been very stable; the speed is constant, and no download has ever stopped or paused. The service is good and has great availability.

Is StargateNX well designed? Yes it is.

It includes a custom theme (the one white and red that can be seen in the pictures above) for SX Installer, which appears by default on the SX Installer options once StargateNX has been placed on the microSD card. The design is clean and tested. Nevertheless, on my first tests I found a few minor, graphical issues, that have been reported, and which will likely be solved with the next update.

Conclusion

It is clear that the switch scene never stops growing and growing, and new innovative products appear in the market. StargateNX is the best product suited for comfort and ease of use, and will satisfy your needs. You can download any package, update games, etc… while having stealth mode activated and not suffering from bans. In addition, it also works if your console is already banned. While I found some minor issues, I hope these will be fixed soon.
